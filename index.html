Who I am

Builder first.
Student second.

I built PhishSentinel because my grandfather got scammed. Battery Bin because nobody put a recycling bin in the right place. Hardware Decoded three times after it got destroyed twice. BrandSnap from one favour that turned into a business. The pattern is always the same.

🔍

Problems first, tools second

Every project started with something I personally witnessed — a grandfather scammed, a landfill overflowing, a community that did not exist. Technology always came second.

🌱

Impact over presentation

75+ kg of toxic batteries not in a landfill. A grandfather who browses confidently. Seven local businesses online. The numbers that matter most do not appear on any certificate.

🎵

Long-term commitment

Flute since Grade 3. Hardware Decoded since Grade 8 — rebuilt after two server raids. The same patience that keeps me at an instrument keeps a project running years after starting it.

⚡

Bias toward action

Most of what I have built was not assigned, not required, not expected. It just seemed worth doing. I do not wait for the right time or someone else to go first.
